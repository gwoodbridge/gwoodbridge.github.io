The Goal

The goal of this project is to recreate a functioning version of Jack Sparrow's Compass as seen in the films Pirates of the Caribbean. The interesting thing about this compass is that it doesn't point north. It points to what you want most.

The Plan

The plan is to use 3D printing for the top and bottom of the compass housing. For the electronics, I plan to use an Arduino Pro Mini Arduino Nano 33 BLE, a micro continuous rotation servo, a GPS reciever, a magnatometer, a Hall Effect Sensor, a 9V battery, and a buck converter. On power up, the Arduino will get it's current position, calculate the heading to the pre-programmed destination, determine north with the magnatometer, and rotate the servo the correct number of degrees to point towards the destination.

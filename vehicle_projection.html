Vehicle Projection

The Goal

The goal of this project is to demonstrate the feasability of projection on the interior of a vehicle to change the vehicle's appearance for various purposes.

The Plan

Projection is often used in theme parks to change or add to the environment in an attraction. However, the ride vehicles are relatively static and don't change based on their environemnt. Projection mapping could be used on the interior of a ride vehicle to change the vehicle's appearance as it goes through different environemnts. This could allow deeper for immersion in the story of the attraction.
